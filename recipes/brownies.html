Brownies

Description

Brownies are the perfect dessert for chocolate-lovers. York Peppermint Patties combine chocolate and mint, an amazing combination. This recipe combines all of the above into a chewy, chocolaty, minty masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups margarine
  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 5 eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 24 small peppermint patties
