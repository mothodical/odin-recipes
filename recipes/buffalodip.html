Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

This dish is a spicy, creamy, delicious masterpiece perfect for an appetizer or a meal. You can dip anything your heart desires into this dip, but top picks include tortilla chips and celery. Perfect for game day or your next group get-together, use this recipe to wow your tastebuds and your friends.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup Frank's RedHot Hot Sauce
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese or blue cheese crumbles

Steps

  1. PREHEAT oven to 350Â°F. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Spoon into shallow 1-quart baking dish.
  2. (Optional) Add a thin layer of extra cheddar cheese or blue cheese on top of mixture.
  3. BAKE 20 minutes or until mixture is heated through; stir. Sprinkle with green onions, if desired, and serve with chips, crackers and/or cut up veggies.
